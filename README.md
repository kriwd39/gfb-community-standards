# Griffon Fauve de Bretagne Community Standards

This repository serves as a living document for the Griffon Fauve de Bretagne community's interpretation of the United Kennel Club (UKC) breed standard. Our primary focus is on preserving the breed's health, welfare, and working ability.

## Purpose

- **Collaborative Stewardship**: Enable transparent discussion and formal adoption of improvements to the breed's interpretive standard.
- **Health and Welfare**: Provide guidelines to avoid exaggerations and conditions detrimental to the dog's well-being and function.
- **Working Preservation**: Emphasize traits essential for the breed's traditional work as a scenthound.

## Repository Structure

- `interpretive-standard.md` – Summary of key characteristics based on UKC standard, with community interpretations.
- `health-and-welfare-guidelines.md` – Best practices for health screening and care.

## How to Contribute

Improvements are proposed via Issues, discussed, and formalized through Pull Requests. Approved changes are merged into the main branch and tagged with releases.

Let's work together to ensure the Griffon Fauve de Bretagne remains a healthy, functional, and true-to-type scenthound.
